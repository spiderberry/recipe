Kimchi Fried Rice

Korean

Dinner

20 minutes

★★★★★ 5 (1 review)

By Daviss Le

A flavorful Korean dish made with seasoned rice, kimchi, and a variety of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked white rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup well-fermented kimchi, chopped
  • 2 tbsp kimchi juice
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, corn, green beans)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 large egg
  • Toasted sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add a little oil and sauté the mixed vegetables and chopped kimchi for about 3 minutes until fragrant.

  2. Step 2

    Add the day-old rice and kimchi juice to the pan. Use a spatula to break up the rice and toss everything together thoroughly.

  3. Step 3

    Continue to stir-fry for another 4-5 minutes until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy at the bottom. Turn off the heat and stir in the sesame oil.

  4. Step 4

    In a separate small pan, fry the egg sunny-side up. Serve the fried rice in a bowl, top with the fried egg, and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
