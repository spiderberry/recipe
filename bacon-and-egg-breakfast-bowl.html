Bacon and Egg Breakfast Bowl

American

Breakfast

25 minutes

4.5 (2 reviews)

By Dandre Kennedy

A hearty breakfast bowl packed with crispy bacon, perfectly scrambled eggs, and fresh vegetables

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon and set it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess grease. Once cooled, crumble the bacon into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Step 2

    In the same skillet, crack the eggs and scramble them until fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the scrambled eggs from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Step 3

    To assemble the breakfast bowl, start with a base of cooked quinoa. Top with scrambled eggs, crumbled bacon, cherry tomatoes, and avocado slices.

  4. Step 4

    Garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
