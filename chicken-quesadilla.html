Chicken Quesadilla

Mexican

Lunch / Dinner

15 minutes

★★★★★ 5 (3 reviews)

By Daviss Le

A delicious Mexican dish made with seasoned chicken, melted cheese, and fresh vegetables in a crispy tortilla shell.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup Mexican cheese blend, shredded
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers and onions, sautéing until they are soft and tender (about 3-4 minutes).

  2. Step 2

    Place one flour tortilla flat in a clean skillet over medium heat. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese evenly over the tortilla.

  3. Step 3

    Spread the shredded chicken and sautéed vegetables over the melting cheese, then top with the remaining cheese. Fold the tortilla in half to cover the filling.

  4. Step 4

    Cook until the bottom is golden brown and crispy (about 2 minutes). Carefully flip the quesadilla and cook the other side until golden. Remove from heat, slice into triangles, and serve with sour cream and salsa!
